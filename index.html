Our Story

We Asked a Simple Question.

"Why does every drink tell you what to taste — but none help you choose how to feel?"

It started with a realization. The beverage industry has spent a century optimizing for flavor, caffeine, and sugar. Energy drinks promise to wire you up. Soft drinks promise refreshment. But nobody was designing drinks around the thing that matters most: how you actually want to feel.

We saw a world where people are more stressed, more overstimulated, and more emotionally disconnected than ever. And we thought: what if a drink could help?

Not as medicine. Not as a supplement. But as a ritual — a conscious, delicious moment where you choose your emotional state through the power of Aroma Therapy and Functional Ingredients.

That's how MOODii was born.

We assembled a team of flavor scientists, aromatherapists, functional ingredient researchers, and consumer psychologists. Together, we created something that has never existed before: the world's first Aroma Therapy Drink.

MOODii isn't just a drink. It's a movement. A belief that you don't have to accept the mood the day gives you. You can choose your own.

The Science

What Is an Aroma Therapy Drink?

MOODii is built on the intersection of four evidence-based pillars that, together, create a beverage experience designed to change how you feel.

01

Aroma Therapy

Your sense of smell is directly connected to your limbic system — the emotional center of the brain. MOODii leverages this by engineering scent profiles that begin working the moment you open the can. Citrus Orange for joy. Lime & Mint for alertness. Lavender & Chamomile for calm.

02

Functional Ingredients

Every MOODii beverage contains a curated blend of natural extracts and bioactive compounds. These are ingredients studied for their ability to support specific emotional and cognitive states — working in harmony with the aroma therapy to amplify the mood shift.

03

Flavor Psychology

Flavor isn't just about taste — it's about association, memory, and emotional response. Each MOODii flavor profile is designed to evoke specific feelings. Warm peach citrus for joy. Clean lime mint for focus. Soft lavender chamomile for rest.

04

Intentional Ritual

The act of choosing a mood — picking up the can, reading the label, taking that first sip — is itself a micro-ritual. Research shows that intentional rituals can measurably shift emotional states. MOODii makes the ritual effortless and enjoyable.

MOODii doesn't promise to cure anything. We promise to give you a delicious, intentional moment that helps you feel more like the version of yourself you want to be — with low sugar, low calories, natural extracts, and no artificial colors.
