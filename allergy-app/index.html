🚨 If a reaction happens

  • Trouble breathing, swelling of lips/tongue/throat, dizziness, or widespread hives can be anaphylaxis — call emergency services immediately.
  • Use an epinephrine auto-injector (EpiPen) right away if one is prescribed. Don't wait to see if it gets better.
  • Lie the person down with legs raised (or sitting up if breathing is hard) and stay with them.
⚠️ AllergyGuard is an educational helper, not medical advice. Ingredient databases and label text can be incomplete or wrong — always read the actual package, ask about cross-contamination, and follow your doctor's guidance.
